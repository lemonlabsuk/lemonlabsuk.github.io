Opensource

scala-uri

scala-uri is is a small Scala library that helps you work with URIs. It's used by over 200 open source projects on github. Click the logos below, to see how these companies are using scala-uri.

  • Mesosphere use scala-uri in Cosmos,the DC/OS Package Manager.

  • The Guardian use scala-uri in a dozen or so projects, including theguardian.com homepage!

  • Snowplow use scala-uri and their users have helped scala-uri better by raising bugs.

  • Graphcool use scala-uri in their stub-server library.

  • Algolia use scala-uri in the scala client for their search platform.

  • Indix use scala-uri in their utils library.
